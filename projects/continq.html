Project

This was a client-based class project. Our client was interested in improving the conversion rate of its flagship SaaS product. Our goal was to identify which users were the most likely to become paying customers, so our client could focus their sales attention on them. With this in mind we conducted ethnographic observations and interviews in order to:

Identify differences between customers that might indicate a key behavior/characteristic to track.

Make recommendations to improve the sales process.
