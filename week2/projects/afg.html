My Role

I’m on a team of 2 for this project. We started in late October 2018.

Project

Research data collection options and recommend one that is user-friendly for a range of technical abilities, yet robust enough to improve reporting.

Organization

Alternatives For Girls (AFG) is a Detroit-based nonprofit serving high-risk girls and young women. They provide critical services including safe shelter, street outreach and educational support, vocational guidance, mentoring, prevention activities, and counseling.

Problem

AFG collects data in multiple ways, based on the preference of the data collectors. They’re looking to synchronize their efforts and use one option that works for everyone. This option needs to offer a robust reporting function, as that is how they measure success, and it should be secure, to protect their clients.

Research

We researched the organization’s current workflow processes by interviewing key stakeholders.

Ideation

We’ve brainstormed several options and narrowed them down to what we think are the best 2-3. We will reach out to the organization to explain these and get some preliminary feedback. Once we’ve settled on the right option for them we will design a new interface, present it to them and explain how it works.

Design Decisions

We have to consider that the users of this process have a wide range of technical ability, so we need something that is easy to use and quick. We want to eliminate unnecessary steps in the process, such as one person entering data on a form and a second person copying that information to another form. The data should all go into one big pot, not separate smaller pots for each department. As AFG is a nonprofit our solution needs to be budget-conscious.
